About Me

My interest in coding started my first semester of graduate school when I had to create a class webpage to post materials and keep students updated with weekly announcements. Designing and building that page was one of the highlights in an otherwise very challenging semester.

The next several years were consumed by finishing my PhD and starting a career in academia but once life calmed down a bit I started thinking about ways to enrich my life. Since I had always loved both art and technology I began looking for a way to purse both of those and found that web development was a perfect blend of the two.

In addition to my career in academia, I have worked and volunteered with a variety of organizations and non-profits. These experiences have given me a unique perspective on what business professionals and clients need out of websites. My diverse professional experiences combined with my passion for tech create a dedicated, knolwedgeable, and passionate web development professional.
